Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about move in/out cleaning

What's included in move out cleaning?

Our move out cleaning includes deep cleaning of all rooms, kitchen (including inside all appliances), bathrooms, floors, windows, baseboards, light fixtures, and more. We follow a comprehensive checklist that covers everything most landlords inspect before returning security deposits.

Do I need to be present during cleaning?

No, you don't need to be present. Many of our clients provide us with keys or access codes and we handle everything. We'll text you updates and photos when we're finished. However, you're welcome to be there if you prefer.

How long does move out cleaning take?

The time varies based on property size and condition. Studio/1 bedroom typically takes 3-4 hours, 2-3 bedrooms take 4-6 hours, and larger homes take 6-8 hours. We'll provide an accurate estimate when you book.

What if my landlord isn't satisfied?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If your landlord or property manager finds any areas that don't meet their standards, we'll return and re-clean those areas at no additional charge. This is our commitment to helping you get your full security deposit back.

Do you provide cleaning supplies and equipment?

Yes! We bring all professional-grade cleaning supplies, equipment, and tools. We use eco-friendly, effective products that are safe for all surfaces. You don't need to provide anything.

Can you clean on short notice?

Yes! We offer same-day and next-day service throughout the DMV region, subject to availability. Call us directly at +012 345 6789 to check same-day availability. We understand moving timelines can be tight and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

What areas do you serve?

We provide move in/out cleaning throughout Virginia (Alexandria, Arlington, Fairfax, Loudoun County, Prince William County), Washington DC (all quadrants), and Maryland (Montgomery County, Prince George's County, Bethesda, Silver Spring, Rockville). Contact us to confirm service in your specific area.

What's the difference between move in and move out cleaning?

Move out cleaning focuses on meeting landlord inspection standards and getting your deposit back. Move in cleaning ensures your new space is perfectly clean and sanitized before you move your belongings in. Both services include deep cleaning, but move out often requires extra attention to areas landlords inspect like appliances and baseboards.

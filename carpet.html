Our Proven Method

The SamstarCleanPro 5-Step Carpet Revival Process

We follow a rigorous process to ensure maximum dirt and stain removal, leaving your carpets truly clean.

1. Inspection

Identify carpet type, fabric, and trouble areas (heavy soiling, stains, damage).

2. Pre-Treatment

Apply eco-friendly pre-spray to break down soil and loosen oil and grease particles.

3. Hot Water Extraction

Deep rinse with powerful steam equipment, extracting dirt, water, and cleaning solution.

4. Spot & Stain Removal

Target any stubborn remaining spots and apply professional-grade stain removers.

5. Post-Grooming

Groom the carpet pile for uniform look and faster drying time (optional protector application).

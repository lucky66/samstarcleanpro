Common Tile & Grout Problems in DMV Homes

Discolored Grout Lines

Grout turns gray, brown, or black from accumulated dirt and mildew, especially in high-traffic areas and bathrooms.

Mold & Mildew Growth

Moisture-prone areas like showers and kitchen backsplashes develop unsightly and unhealthy mold in grout lines.

Embedded Dirt & Stains

Years of foot traffic, spills, and daily use create stubborn stains that regular mopping can't remove.

Dull, Lifeless Tiles

Soap scum, hard water deposits, and film buildup make tiles look dingy and lackluster.
